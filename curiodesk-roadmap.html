Product Architecture · SAP AMS Operations Platform

curioDesk is a base platform.
The margin is in the modules.

The base handles ticketing, SLAs, onboarding and reporting — table stakes every AMS provider needs. Everything past that is an add-on module sold on top. And they all stand on one thing: a SAP-aware credentials vault and CMDB. Health checks, SOD scans, patch comparison and the AI agent can't touch a customer system without it — so the vault isn't a peer module, it's the substrate.
