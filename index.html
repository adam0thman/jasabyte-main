Enterprise AI

AI that can touch SAP — safely.

Most enterprise AI initiatives die in the security review. Ours is designed for it.

"The model proposes.
A deterministic gate disposes."

The LLM never holds the keys. Every action it suggests passes through a plain-code policy enforcement point that decides against a verified identity — so a jailbroken prompt cannot escalate. We prove it with a red-team test suite.

  • Verified identity. Microsoft Entra sign-in; roles derived from security-group claims.
  • Read-only into SAP. Real RFC access to S/4HANA — dumps, jobs, locks, queues. Writes are forbidden by design.
  • Local-first. Runs on your infrastructure with an on-prem model. Your data never leaves.
  • Swappable brain. Local model by default; any hosted model with one config line when policy allows.
